# Privacy Policy

<p dateTime="2026-01-17" className="text-caption text-muted-foreground">Last updated: Jan 17, 2026</p>

We value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use [https://bl-labs.vercel.app](https://bl-labs.vercel.app) (the "Website"), how we use it, and the choices you may have.

## Information we collect

We may collect the following types of information:

- **Information you provide** (for example, when you submit a contact or quote form): name, phone number, email address, and any details you include in your message
- **Information collected automatically** when you browse the Website: IP address, device identifiers, browser information, pages viewed, approximate location (derived from IP), and interaction data.

## How we use information

We use information we collect to:

- Respond to inquiries and provide customer support
- Provide quotes and perform requested services
- Improve and secure the Website
- Measure performance and understand how visitors use the Website
- Advertise our services and measure ad performance

## Cookies and similar technologies

We and our partners may use cookies, pixels, tags, and similar technologies to:

- Understand Website traffic and usage
- Remember preferences
- Measure the effectiveness of advertising
- Help prevent fraud and improve security

Most browsers let you control cookies through your settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect Website functionality.

## Advertising and conversion measurement

We use **Google Analytics** and **Google Ads** to measure Website traffic, user interactions, and the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns.

In some cases, we may use **enhanced conversion measurement**, which allows us to securely share **hashed** customer information (such as an email address or phone number you provide during a form submission) with Google for the sole purpose of improving conversion attribution. This information is hashed before transmission and is used in accordance with Google’s policies.

You can learn more about how Google uses data from sites that use its services here:

[policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites)

For more information on how Google collects and processes data, please review Google’s Privacy Policy:

[policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)


If you would like to opt out of Google Analytics, you can use Google’s opt-out browser add-on:

[tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout/)


We may also use **Microsoft Clarity** and **Microsoft Advertising** to understand how visitors interact with our Website (including behavioral metrics and session replay) and for advertising. Learn more via the [Microsoft Privacy Statement](https://privacy.microsoft.com/en-us/privacystatement).

## With whom we share information

We may share limited information with trusted third-party service providers that help us operate the Website, measure performance, and advertise our services (including analytics, advertising, and measurement providers such as Google and Microsoft). These providers process information on our behalf for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy.

We do **not** sell your personal information.

## Data retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide services, operate the Website, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

## Children’s privacy

The Website is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

## Do Not Track

Some browsers offer a “Do Not Track” (“DNT”) signal. Because there is no consistent industry standard for responding to DNT signals, we do not respond to them.
